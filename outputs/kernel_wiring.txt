IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Type bridge: ExecTool::new() requires Arc<tokio::sync::Mutex<AccessManager>>,
  but the kernel's access_manager is Arc<parking_lot::Mutex<AccessManager>>.
  Solution: clone the inner AccessManager into a fresh tokio::sync::Mutex.
  This means the two mutexes are NOT the same instance — changes to one
  won't be reflected in the other. For Phase 2, consider either:
  (a) switching kernel.rs to tokio::sync::Mutex uniformly, or
  (b) making ExecTool generic over the mutex type.

- Pre-existing compile errors in agent_runtime.rs / program_tool.rs are
  unrelated to these changes (ProgramTool::from_definition signature mismatch).
  Zero errors in kernel.rs.
